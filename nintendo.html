1. The Nintendo Experience

Why Choose Nintendo?

You don't buy a Nintendo console for ultra-realistic 4K graphics; you buy it because it is the only place you can play legendary franchises like Mario, Zelda, and Pokémon. Nintendo is built around the "Hybrid" concept: the console is a tablet that you can plug into your living room TV for a big-screen experience, or instantly pull out and take on an airplane to play anywhere in the world.
