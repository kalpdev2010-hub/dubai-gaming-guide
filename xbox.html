1. The Xbox Experience

Why Choose Microsoft?

Xbox is designed to be the "Netflix of Gaming." While they have powerful consoles, their biggest selling point is Xbox Game Pass—a subscription that gives you instant access to hundreds of high-quality games, including brand-new releases on day one. If you want maximum variety for a flat monthly fee rather than buying individual 250 AED games, Xbox is the undisputed champion.
