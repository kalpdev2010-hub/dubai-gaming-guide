1. The PlayStation Experience

Why Choose Sony?

PlayStation is built for people who want highly polished, blockbuster movies that they can actually play. Sony’s exclusive games are visually stunning and focus heavily on deep storylines. Furthermore, their DualSense Controller is revolutionary, featuring haptic feedback (you can feel the rain falling in your hands) and adaptive triggers (a bowstring gets harder to pull as you draw it back).
